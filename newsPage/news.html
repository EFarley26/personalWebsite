Summer Internship @ Xenon Arc

Over my 2024 summer break, I had the wonderful opportunity to work with Xenon Arc as an Engineering Software Intern. Xenon Arc is a chemical and food ingredient distributor and reseller that helps large organizations reach and serve the SMB market with personalized support to drive growth. I had the amazing chance to put my HTML, JavaScript, and CSS skills to work when I helped redesigned their Conntact product, a website intended to drive company growth and sales for customers. I also built skills in Microsoft Dynamics 365 when I supported their customer data migration and created new data import processes. I learned so much from my colleagues at Xenon Arc and had a fantastic time.

Grand Challenges Scholars Program

I am thrilled to announce that I have been accepted into the Notre Dame Grand Challenges Scholars Program. The program helps prepare engineering students to tackle pressing global challenges through interdisciplinary education, research, entrepreneurship, global experiences, and service learning. I have chosen to focus on the intersection of technology and the education industry where I believe AI can revolutionize the way students are taught and lessons are developed by helping each student learn in a more individualized way that best suits their needs. Leveraging technology in new ways will also help us address the growing teacher-student challenges.

As an Engineering Grand Challenges honors scholar, I explore the intersection of AI and VR/XR in the education industry, aiming to create transformative experiences through cutting-edge technology that give students a more personalize and effective learning journey.
